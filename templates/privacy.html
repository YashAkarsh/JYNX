Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 22, 2023

This Privacy Policy outlines the privacy practices of JYNX Company and details the management of personal information collected through our application, website, and other related services (collectively referred to as the “Services”).

Overview:

JYNX facilitates the receipt of anonymous messages by users through posts on our website. The process involves Website Users creating posts, either directly or anonymously, which can be used to solicit messages from individuals with access to the provided link (referred to as “Message Senders”).

Upon clicking the JYNX post, Message Senders can view the post in detail and send messages directly to the Website User. The Website User also has the option to share anonymous messages and responses on their Instagram account. It is important to note that JYNX does not store the name or Instagram handle of Message Senders and cannot disclose this information to Website Users or any third parties. However, JYNX may reveal certain properties of the Message Sender to the Website User based on the Message Sender’s IP address or browser user agent. Additionally, such information may be shared for compliance, safety, and integrity purposes.

Information Collection:

Information Collection:
We collect information through various sources depending on user interactions with our Services, including:
  • Account information provided by Website Users during sign-up
  • Messages submitted by Message Senders to Website Users.
  • Feedback, correspondence, and other information provided when users contact us with inquiries or engage with us online.
  • Usage information, encompassing interactions with the Services and user-provided content.
  • Other information collected in accordance with this Privacy Policy or as disclosed during collection.
  • Information obtained from third-party sources, such as marketing partners, publicly-available platforms, lead generators, and data providers.
  • Automatic data collection tools, including cookies, local storage technologies, and web beacons, to log information about user devices, interactions, and online activities.

How Personal Information is Used

We utilize personal information for the following purposes:
  • Providing and supporting the Services, including user account creation, responding to requests, customer support, and sending announcements and updates.
  • Improving, monitoring, personalizing, and protecting our Services and communications, involving troubleshooting, testing, research, security measures, and investigating and protecting against fraudulent or unauthorized activities.
  • Marketing and advertising, encompassing direct marketing communications, newsletters, promotions, and interest-based advertising.
  • Enforcing agreements, complying with legal obligations, and defending against legal claims or disputes.

Sharing of Personal Information:

Personal information may be shared with:
  • Service providers assisting in the operation of our Services, such as customer support, content moderation, hosting, analytics, and payment processing.
  • Advertising partners for interest-based advertising.
  • Third parties for compliance, fraud prevention, and safety purposes.
  • In the event of business transfers, where some or all of our business or assets, including personal information, may be sold, transferred, or shared.

User Rights and Choices:

Users have the right to:
  • Obtain information about the collection and use of personal information.
  • Access a copy of the collected personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated personal information.
  • Request the deletion of personal information no longer needed for Services or other lawful purposes.
  • Exercise additional rights, such as objecting to or restricting the use of personal information and withdrawing consent where applicable.
  • Requests can be made through the provided contact information, subject to limitations based on legal obligations or the rights of others.

Children’s Privacy:

Our Services are not intended for users under 13 years of age. If personal information is inadvertently collected from a child under 13, it will be promptly deleted upon becoming aware of the situation.

International Data Transfers:

As a company based in India, personal information may be transferred to affiliates and service providers in India and other jurisdictions. Safeguards are implemented to ensure compliance with data protection laws.

While efforts are made to secure personal information, complete security cannot be guaranteed during data transmission over the Internet.
